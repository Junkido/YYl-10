BLACKPINK Story

• On June 29, 2016, YG Entertainment announced the launch of a new girl group, marking the birth of a girl group following 2NE1 after 7 years. It was not until August 8, 2016, that the 4 BLACKPINK girls officially debuted in public.

• At that time, although they had just debuted, BLACKPINK performed very impressively and quickly dominated the music charts of the domestic and foreign markets. After the group started to be notified of its activity, all information about the group was searched all over the web. The fan of the 4 girls also gradually increased exponentially.

    BLACKPINK members: Jisoo, Rosé, Jennie and Lisa
    Leader of BLACKPINK: The special thing about the group is that there is no leader, each member has a clear mission and always supports each other.
    BLACKPINK's fandom name: BLINK
    Lightstick BLACKPINK: Designed with two main colors, Pink and Black, shaped like a toy hammer and called Hammer Bong.
