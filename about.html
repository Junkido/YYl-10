About Me

Lalisa Manobal

Lalisa Manobal (born Pranpriya Manobal; born 27 March 1997), better known by her stage name Lisa, is a Thai rapper, singer, songwriter and dancer. She is the youngest member of the Korean girl group Blackpink under YG Entertainment.

Lisa made her solo debut with the single album Lalisa in September 2021, with the title single of the same name. The album sold over 736,000 copies in its week of release in Korea, making her the first female artist to do so. The music video for the title single of the same name garnered 73.6 million views on YouTube in the first 24 hours of release and became the most viewed music video in the first 24 hours on the platform by an artist. soloist. The song earned Lisa two Guinness World Records and the first MTV Video Music Award by a K-pop solo artist in history.
